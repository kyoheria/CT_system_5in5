What is 5 in 5

5 in 5 is an assignmet that was assigned in CT Lab: System to make a daily sketch that shows some kind of intraction with a a small set of constraints to guide the conceptualizaon of daily sketches.

"Sketches are artisticc expressions not intended as finished works. Sketching serves many purposes and a practice of frequent sketching is extremely valuable to arsts and designers." - from Assignmet sheet

we started this process by makeing a mind map of what we like and what we are interested in and I came up with a topic of

creating a space

and for the constraints, I desided to

One sketch a day for a 5 days

Choose two key word from the mind map we made in the class.

Make a new mind map with those keyword or idea that came across by choosing those keywords

Make some kind of sketch that shows a interaction

Make a moodboard to communicate idea more clearly
